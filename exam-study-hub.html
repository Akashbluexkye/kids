📝 This & That

👆 This = close to me! (near)

"This is my toy!" – point to something near you

"This is my book." – the book is in your hand

👉 That = far away! (far)

"That is a tree!" – point to something far

"That is a bird." – the bird is on the roof

📦 in, on, under

🔵 IN = inside something

The ball is in the box. 📦

The book is in the bag. 🎒

🟢 ON = on top of something

The book is on the table. 📚

The cat is on the mat. 🐱

🟡 UNDER = below something

The ball is under the chair. 🪑

The dog is under the table. 🐕
